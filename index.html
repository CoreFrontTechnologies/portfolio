About Me

Hello! I'm Sayyeed Muhammad, a passionate front-end developer with 1 year of experience in creating visually appealing and user-friendly web interfaces. I specialize in crafting clean, efficient, and responsive designs that enhance the user experience. As the owner of CoreFront Technologies, I lead a team committed to delivering top-notch front-end development services. My expertise lies in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I continually strive to stay updated with the latest trends and technologies in the field. In addition to my professional work, I founded EduStack Hub, a growing community focused on full stack development. Through my work with CoreFront Technologies and EduStack Hub, I aim to blend creativity with technical proficiency to build websites that not only look great but also perform seamlessly. I'm excited to collaborate with clients and peers to create innovative solutions that stand out. Thank you for visiting my portfolio.
